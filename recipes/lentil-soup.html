Lentil Soup

Description:

Nothing beats a good lentil soup in cold days, or in warm days... or everyday!

This version of lentil soup involves a bit of work, but the end result is worth it. I can guarantee it.

Ingredients:

  • 250 grams of clean lentils
  • Two finely chopped tomatoes
  • 1/3 of a big onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 tablespoon of oil of your preferences
  • 1 small bottle of tomato sauce
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 150 grams of bacon
  • 1 medium pieces of chorizo
  • 250 grams of sausages
  • 3 boiled and peeled eggs
  • 1 ripe plantain (or plátano macho)
  • A bunch of Cilantro (optional)

Steps:

  1. In a pan, put the butter and oil so the butter doesn't burn and cook the chopped onion and tomatoes. Reserve.
  2. Chop the bacon, the sausages and the plantain. Cook the bacon and sausages and reserve.
  3. In a big pot, boil the lentils until they're soft.
  4. Add the tomato sauce, onion, tomatoes, bacon, sausages, the eggs and plantain into the lentil pot. If you want, add cilantro for extra flavor and cook for a bit more until the plantain is soft.
  5. Serve your portions in a bowl and enjoy!
